WEC Health & Safety Action Guide

This guide presents practical advice and valuable resources for solving health and safety problems. It includes information on what workers and unions can do and where to go for outside help. It also including sample letters you can use to contact various agencies.

In addition to the guide, WEC has also produced a poster and wallet card.
